From Paypal to Tesla and SpaceX to Mars

Multi-time Founder

Digital payments are embeded in everything we do. We can now swipe or tap our phones to pay for things automatically. While there is the question of security, digital payments are here to stay and they will become more intricate. Paying digitally wasn't necessarily a new concept but no one made it work better than paypal did.

Paypal came from a merger of Musk's second company, X.com, with Confinity. Confinity was founded by Peter Thiel, Max Levchin and Luke Nosek. Originally Confinity was founded to be a Palm Pilot payment and cryptography company. X.com was founded to do email pbased payments. Once merged they worked on digital payments as a whole.
